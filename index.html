I worked on this project with a group from my bootcamp. This site is for people who are looking to move to another country or to travel, it allows someone to quickly look up a country and gives them specific information about that country. We got it up and working, for future editing, we wanted to add a section where a person could create an account and save their search history, and then be able to compare countries together so they can see the main dfferences. We also would like to add a certain page that would allow the user to fill in informaton (like whats is most important to them in a country) and then give them recommendatons on where they may like to go.
